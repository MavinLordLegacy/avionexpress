Why Avion Express?
In today's discerning environment, cluttered with many brands and choices, few organisations are flexible enough to offer the promise of Speed and Reliability at an affordable price. The constituents of the Avion Express values are to professionally deliver your demands with our own special brand of care and attention.
 

Diverse Solutions

With over 41 years of cumulative experience in the logistics industry, we are able to configure optimal solutions for your every distribution need. Whether it be in the air, on the road, at sea or standing still, we'll find the right answer at the right price.

 

Online Interface

Avion Express offers a superior online interface that allows for access to your distribution needs, in booking a collection, tracking your shipment, administering your account and many more features. However, if it's the human touch you want, our call centre agents are just a call away in the traditional way. Give us a call on 0860 SHIP IT.

 

Our partners

Integral to the success of our business is the effective selection of our logistics partners who integrate with us. In this way, we provide an offering that blends the power of infrastructure, and our personal and professional expertise in configuring the optimal solution for you.
